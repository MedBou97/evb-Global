# **App Name**: Apex Consulting

## Core Features:

- Agency Details Display: Display comprehensive information about the consulting agency, including its mission, vision, values, and history.
- Service Showcase: Present the agency's key services with detailed descriptions and client success stories.
- Team Introduction: Introduce the consulting team members with their photos, bios, and areas of expertise.
- Header Navigation: Implement a clear and intuitive header navigation with a dropdown list for each header section.
- Client Testimonials: Display client testimonials and reviews to build trust and credibility.
- Contact Information: Provide clear contact information and a contact form for inquiries and business development.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#2E3A59) to convey professionalism and trust.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), nearly white, to ensure readability and a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#D4AF37) to highlight important elements and add a touch of elegance.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif font, to present a precise and contemporary style for all headings.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font, to guarantee comfortable readability throughout the site.
- Use simple, professional line icons to visually represent services and key information.
- Clean, grid-based layout for a structured and easy-to-navigate experience.
- Subtle hover effects on navigation items and buttons to enhance user interaction.
